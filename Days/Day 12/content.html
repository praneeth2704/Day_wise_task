Description

"Jujutsu Kaisen" (JJK) is a popular anime series based on the manga of the same name written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. The story revolves around Yuji Itadori, a high school student with impressive physical abilities who stumbles upon a cursed object, a severed finger, containing a powerful curse. When his friends accidentally release the curse, Yuji swallows the finger to protect them, thus becoming the host of Sukuna, the King of Curses.
